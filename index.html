There Is No Third Corridor

Houthi missiles struck the Saudi tanker Encelia in the Red Sea corridor Riyadh built to survive the closure of Hormuz. Pipeline capacity is fixed, Hormuz is shut, and the last exit runs past Yemen. The blockade does not need to stop Saudi shipping. It needs to price it — and tanker counts at Bab el-Mandeb suggest it has started.
