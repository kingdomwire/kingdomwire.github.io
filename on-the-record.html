Saudi government ministers, the royal court, and government entities in their own words.

SPA
Saudi Press Agency
Official statement · Tanker attacks
22 Jul 2026 · Riyadh
"…a violation of international laws and conventions that guarantee the safety of commercial vessels and their crews."
The kingdom's first response to the Houthi strikes on the tankers Encelia and Layla. The language stays in the register of maritime law rather than retaliation — consistent with the posture Riyadh has held since spring, and notable precisely because the attacks tested it directly.

MFA
Ministry of Foreign Affairs
On the Houthi blockade declaration
21 Jul 2026 · Riyadh
"…all necessary measures to protect its ships."
Issued after the blockade declaration and a day before missiles struck the Encelia. The phrase leaves every option open while committing to none. Which measures, and whose navy provides them, is now the operative question of the Red Sea corridor.

MFA
Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Joint readout · Europe calls
22 Jul 2026 · Riyadh
"The ministers emphasized the importance of efforts to preserve maritime security in the Red Sea and the Arabian Gulf, and rejected any actions that endanger safe navigation."
A day of separate calls — Albares, Tajani, Wadephul — two days after the Houthi blockade declaration. The ministry's own framing accuses the Houthis of imposing a maritime blockade on the Kingdom and asks for implementation of Security Council Resolutions 2216 and 2722. The diplomacy is aimed where a declared blockade bites first: European insurers, shippers and the governments behind them.

CAB
Council of Ministers
Chaired by King Salman
21 Jul 2026 · Jeddah
"…the importance of an immediate halt to all forms of military escalation, to preserve the security and stability of the region."
The same session reaffirmed support for Kuwait, Bahrain and Jordan over Iranian violations. Solidarity with the neighbors taking hits, paired with a call to stop the escalation: the two-track posture, restated at the top of government in a week when the war reached Saudi shipping lanes.

MFA
Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Call with Secretary Rubio
11 Jul 2026
"Continuing coordination and joint consultation to achieve security and stability in the region."
The readout disclosed nothing beyond the formula, and the formula is the point: the Washington channel runs on schedule through a war month. It landed the same day as a cabinet reshuffle that handed Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman a combined energy-industry portfolio — a continuity message on both tracks.

MFA
Ministry of Foreign Affairs
Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
14 Jul 2026 · Riyadh
"The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia expresses its condemnation and strongest denunciation of Iran's continued destabilizing conduct, which undermines the security and stability of the region and violates the principles of international law and the Charter of the United Nations."
Issued following Iranian attacks on Kuwaiti border posts, Emirati tankers in the Strait of Hormuz, and diplomatic missions across the Gulf. The Foreign Minister separately held calls with counterparts in Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Jordan and the US Secretary of State.
